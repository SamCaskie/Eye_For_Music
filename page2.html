1994-1996: The Golden Age of CD Art

By the mid-1990s, CDs had fully taken over, and cover art became a crucial part of an artist's identity. The rise of digital design tools allowed for more experimental artwork leading to some of the most memorable covers such as: PHAT JAZZ Flava ‘95 and CrazySexyCool.
