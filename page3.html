1997-1999: The Digital Revolution Begins

As the internet began to influence music culture, CD covers started to reflect a blend of traditional and digital aesthetics. This era saw bright and bold designs on covers like: Spice World and Underdogs. CD's started to decline during this time, as MP3s and file-sharing emerged.
