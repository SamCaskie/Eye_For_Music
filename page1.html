1990-1993: The Dawn of CD Dominance

The early 1990s marked the transition from cassette tapes to CDs. This era saw iconic album covers from genres like grunge, hip-hop, and rock, reflecting the raw energy and DIY ethos of the time. Showcasing CDs like Arested development, Whats the 411, and DAS EFX.
